Virtual ICU Visit Management Portal is a secure and compassionate digital healthcare solution designed to help family members stay connected with ICU patients when physical visits are restricted. Through OTP-based login and scheduled video sessions, families can easily request virtual visits, while nurses and hospital staff can manage approvals, prioritize critical patients, and control visit timing efficiently. This system helps reduce the emotional stress and anxiety families often face during critical care situations by ensuring timely communication and virtual presence. It also supports hospitals by minimizing ICU crowding, maintaining infection-control protocols, reducing manual coordination efforts, and improving transparency in the visit process. By enabling safe, organized, and policy-compliant communication, the portal strengthens trust between healthcare providers and families, improves patient-family satisfaction, and ensures compassionate care beyond physical boundaries.
